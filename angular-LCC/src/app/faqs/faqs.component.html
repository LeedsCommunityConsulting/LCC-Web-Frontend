LCC is a student-led pro-bono consultancy firm.

Our aims include:

  • enabling students to gain real world experience first-hand
  • providing a positive social impact to the local Leeds community

We aim to provide value in our local community. We attract the most intelligent and enthusiastic minds in Leeds and match them with clients that come to us with a variety of issues and aims!

  • Free yet professional services
  • Access to fresh perspective and creative minds of students
  • Wide variety of skill sets brought to the project

To view our past clients, go to our clients page.

Leeds Community Consulting is a pro-bono consultancy to help students acquire the skills necessary to pursue a future in consultancy. Our members therefore do not earn a salary.
